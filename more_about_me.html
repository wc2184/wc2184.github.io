Powdered Food Experiment

One of the biggest causes of overeating is boredom and the desire to feel good for a short time. During the 2020 Covid-19 pandemic, most people were quarantining from society for many months in a row. So, naturally, people would want to eat more and thus many people gained weight! I gained 30 lbs (13.6 kgs) last year! So, I wanted to go on a diet.

But one of the main issues of trying to diet was that people did not have accurate calorie counters. My mom's homecooked chinese dinners would have anywhere from 1000 calories to 2000 calories but I wouldn't know how much, because it is homecooked.

Enter, Soylent

What is Soylent?
Well, Soylent is simply a complete powdered food. "Complete" means that this powder has all the nutrients you need to survive, and you just need to consume this one food and all your calories, vitamins, minerals, sodium, etc... will be fulfilled. It is a powder you mix with water in a shaker bottle that becomes liquid food. Takes 30 seconds to make.

Trip to Disney World

Photo by The Daily Beast News, licensed under Creative Commons Attribution 2.0 Generic (CC BY 2.0).

Disney World is the paradise of many kid's dreams. It is a entertainment resort that has rides, restuarants, swimming pools, costumed childhood characters, and so much more. I was fortunate my parents could afford to let me visit Disney when I was 10 years old. I spent a week and visited the 4 parks of Disney which included the Animal Kingdom which had elephants, giraffes, lions, pumas, and much more. I loved seeing these animals as a kid and won't ever forget these memories.

Some of the animals I saw:

Photo by Chicago Tribune, licensed under Creative Commons Attribution 2.0 Generic (CC BY 2.0).

Overall, the trip was so fun and I want to back as an adult. There's something magical about seeing animals outside of a zoo, as the Animal Kingdom is more of a safari-type exhibition. I never knew giraffes were so tall! Maybe I'll go back after the Covid-19 pandemic is over and Disney starts reopening in full! I highly suggest checking out Disney World's website, just click here.
