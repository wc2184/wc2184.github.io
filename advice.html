Perfection is cancer, just fucking wing it.
(A C+ is literally better than whatever fuck extreme procrastination/overworrying/nearly failing out of college bullshit you do.)

No one is coming. No one is coming to tell you what to do.

Everything will be okay- trust the soup. Disconnect from the internet for a bit.

Act immediately - Don't even allow 1 second of worry or analysis or planning.

Start sloppy & Find what's the next, smallest, actionable step, and just do that, right now.
e.g. This essay is so hard... Okay. I'm focused on writing the first sentence. Just the first sentence. Can't fail here.

Be present, stop thinking about the future or past.
Oh? That kid bullied you at age 10 - oh you need a hug my sweet young boy - Shut the fuck up - self-pity and blame is a waste of energy. We are living in the present, the past literally does not exist.

Nothing you've ever done makes you a bad person. You were the product of your environment. Be vulnerable. You are inherently worth it as a human being no matter what you think or do.

Make a designated/scheduled worrying time. So you don't worry now, it's not time yet. Contract all the pain into just 1 hour a day, instead of worrying 24/7.
(CBT Technique)

Take the steps to do what you should do. After that, overthinking or worrying is akin to retardation, like suffering hundreds of times again and again over one thing unnecessarily.

You can worry, and try to do work while suffering- or you can worry less, and do work while minimizing your suffering. Nothing changes about your uncontrollable external circumstances in either choice.

Work like fucking hell. 10+ hours a day. 7 days a week. 💪

"Shhh.." your mind in every moment... when walking, when using PC, when in class, even when laying in bed reading emails... Just "Shhh.." - yeah, it feels good to not have a noisy brain.

You don't have to get it perfect, you just have to get it going. Babies don't walk the first time they try- but eventually they get it right.

So say it. So do it. You want to indulge in that cancerous action? So do it. Just do it then- since you want to so badly.
