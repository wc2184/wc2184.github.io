Some recommendations below:

How can Podcasts improve your wellness?

Just being able to listen to people who are experts in their field regarding how mental health works- this is just an amazing thing in modern society. It's like a free therapy session from your home. You don't just intake knowledge from these people, but you genuinely can internalize some of the good life habits and mindsets that they talk about which undoubtedly improve yourself. So many of us humans are lost today without guidance and we feel alone in this hectic, crazy world- it's amazing to just step back and listen to dialouges which cheer you up and can change your life- podcasts certainly changed both my physical and mental wellbeing.

My personal favorite podcasters:

I really love listening to any speaker who goes into the most vulnerable aspects of human life. Brene Brown is the queen of vulnerability- so I love listening to her. I also love listening to Oprah, since she's Oprah. I regularly listen to Buddhists monks like Thich Nhat Hanh or meditators like Sam Harris who practice mindfulness/meditation and have totally changed my life. Dr. K from HealthyGamerGG is also quite the relateable character- helping people understand mental health from a dumbed-down family friendly perspective. Tom Bilyeu also regularly has guests on that talk about mental health alot. I love all these people.
