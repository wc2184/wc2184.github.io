How can Podcasts improve your mind?

It's simple. You obtain massive amounts of wisdom from other people. You effectively save yourself from doing the hard work, and let people research things for you at no personal cost to yourself. It's awesome. You gain knowledge with less effort as well- you can listen to a podcast while doing chores, napping, working, or even playing games. It's great to be living in a time where you can listen to some of the smartest people on Earth speak at the hands of your computer or mobile phone.

My personal favorite podcasters:

I really love listening to people all over the political spectrum. Though I stand heavily progressive, it's still interesting to hear dialouge from all sides of the aisle. Because "Mind" podcasts don't necessarily have to involve politics, I often ignore the political sides of guests and focus on the intellectual aspects instead, skipping entire episodes if I have to in order to avoid the politics. I listen to alot of Naval Ravikant, Lex Fridman, Sam Harris, Joe Rogan, and many others.
